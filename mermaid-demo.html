üéØ Tips for Best Results

  • Keep it simple: Complex diagrams are harder to read
  • Use styles: Color-code important nodes (see examples above)
  • Labels matter: Use clear, descriptive text
  • Test rendering: Use mermaid.live to preview
  • Mobile-friendly: Don't make diagrams too wide
  • Font sizing: Default 16px works well with Patrick Hand
